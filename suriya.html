Suriya

Saravanan Sivakumar (born 23 July 1975[2][3]), known professionally as Suriya, is an Indian actor and film producer who primarily works in Tamil films. One of the highest-paid Tamil actors,[4][5] Suriya is considered as one of the finest actors of Indian cinema.[6][7] Suriya has received numerous accolades including two National Film Awards, six Filmfare Awards South and five Tamil Nadu State Film Awards.[8][9] He has featured six times in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list.
